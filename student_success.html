Student Success at UMSI

Get the Support you need at UMich

At the University of Michigan, your academic success and well-being are our top priorities. We understand that navigating university life can come with challenges, whether they are academic, personal, or professional. This page is designed to help you access the essential resources that will support you throughout your academic journey.

If you ever feel unsure about how to utilize these resources, or if you have any questions, don’t hesitate to contact your Advising Team. They’re here to guide you and help you make the most of all the support available.
