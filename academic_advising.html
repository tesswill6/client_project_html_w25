Academic Advising at UMSI

Find your Home at Michigan

At the University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI), academic advising is a collaborative process designed to support your academic, professional, and personal growth. Our team of experienced, knowledgeable, and approachable academic advisors is here to help you navigate your journey and make the most out of your time at UMSI.

What is Academic Advising?

Academic advising at UMSI is a dynamic and personalized experience where you, as a student, work closely with a qualified UMSI Academic Advisor to craft a plan that fits your aspirations. It's not just about choosing courses; it's about envisioning your future, overcoming challenges, and staying on track to meet your long-term goals. Together, you’ll develop a plan that aligns with both your academic requirements and your personal ambitions.
