UMSI Peer Tutoring Program

In partnership with our UMSI faculty, the UMSI Peer Tutoring program is an opportunity for UMSI students and students taking UMSI courses to engage with peer tutors and receive assistance and support in UMSI courses. Get the help you need from a student that might be able to help you understand the problems more than a professor would.
